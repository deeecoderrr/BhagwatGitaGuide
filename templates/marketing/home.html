FAQ — ITR computation & income tax computation summary

What is ITR computation?
The calculation of income under each head, deductions, taxes, refund or demand for an assessment year—often summarized as a computation sheet for filing or CA review.
What is an income tax computation summary?
A structured walkthrough of gross income, deductions, tax liability, TDS, and refund or demand—useful for CA review and assessment-year records.
Does this replace a CA or the income-tax portal?
No. We import filed ITR-3 JSON and help you generate a reviewed summary PDF—professional advice and official filing remain your responsibility.
Which file format is supported?
Filed ITR-3 JSON from the income-tax portal (acknowledgment / utility export).
How long can I download each summary PDF?
For the retention window shown on the site (default {% itr_output_retention_hours %} hours). Save a copy locally before expiry.
Who uses an ITR computation PDF?
Professionals and filers preparing assessment-year paperwork, refunds, demands, or internal folders alongside notices or bank documentation.
What is the difference between a computation summary and filed ITR JSON?
Your filed return JSON is structured data exported from the income-tax portal. This app reads that JSON and renders a readable computation-summary PDF layout for review—not a substitute for filing or statutory forms.
Is this the same as advance tax computation or self-assessment tax?
Computation summaries here reflect figures parsed from filed ITR-3 JSON (including liability, TDS, relief, refund, demand). Separate advance-tax calculators focus on installments before filing.
