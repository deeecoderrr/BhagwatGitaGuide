Delete Account Request

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data for Ask, Meditate, Inner Peace (Ask Bhagavad Gita) by contacting: {{ support_email }}

How to request deletion

  • Send an email to {{ support_email }}.
  • Use subject: Delete My Account.
  • Include your registered account email/username so we can locate your account.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile data linked to your user account.
  • Saved app content associated with your account (for example: conversation history and saved reflections), except where retention is required by law.
  • Associated notification device registrations linked to your account.

What may be retained and why

  • Limited billing/transaction records may be retained for legal, accounting, anti-fraud, and compliance obligations.
  • Security and abuse-prevention logs may be retained for a limited period.
We process verified account deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe. If we must retain specific records for legal/compliance reasons, those records are isolated and retained only as required.
